MONEY HEIST

Set in Madrid, a mysterious man known as the "Professor" recruits a group of eight people, who choose city names as their aliases, to carry out an ambitious plan that involves entering the Royal Mint of Spain, and escaping with €984 million.

This time, our antiheroes are up against the army, who've been tasked to take down every thief they run into at the Bank of Spain. When we last saw our heist squad, things ended with a bang — literally. But in the unresolved aftermath, viewers might struggle to figure out exactly where each character is.
